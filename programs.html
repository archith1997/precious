    Caring for the Community

    Caring for the Community drives to collect food, toys and clothes for those less fortunate. These drives will be conducted monthly. If you are interested in contributing to these drives, please visit the donation page or contact us at connect@pbfamilyfoundation.org

    BETs

    BETs (Birdsong’s Educational Talks) will consist of influential people within the Chattanooga metropolitan area speaking with young aspiring professionals between the ages of 14-22 (exceptions to be made).

    Motivational BETs will occur periodically throughout the year and will be community service events giving young professionals an opportunity to get involved in the community by devoting their time to other activities/organizations in need of assistance. BETs are not limited to a specific gender, ethnicity, or race unless otherwise specified. BETs may take place at high schools in the Chattanooga area, as we’d collaborate with these schools to provide a professional development experience with educational content.

    Scholarship

    To apply for this scholarship, a student/family must have already been granted acceptance into the Baylor School.

    With providing scholarships, it would be in the efforts of the Precious Birdsong Family Foundation to improve the children’s educational and life outcomes. While the criteria for the scholarship(s) are still being finalized, the recipients must meet standard testing requirements and fulfill a community service obligation. The Precious Birdsong Family Foundation will strive to redefine what a community and family looks like, and as we do so, we will continue to break barriers and open doors for the students and families in Chattanooga.

    Some of the criteria is as follows:

    • Academic Success – GPA requirement of 3.0
    • Financial Need
    • Participation in extra-curricular activities/sports
    • Personal Character
    • Leadership Roles
