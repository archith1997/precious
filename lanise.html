    La’Nise Baker-Calhoun

    Board Member

    La’Nise Baker-Calhoun is a Packing Tech at McKee Foods Corporation. She currently resides in Chattanooga, TN where she is the mother of three sons and one daughter and a grandmother to ten grandchildren.

    La’Nise holds a few certifications within the Tennessee Early Childhood Training Alliance including Infant/Toddler Orientation, Administrator Orientation, and a certificate of starting her own Child Care Business.

    Passionate About

    • Helping and supporting children to believe in themselves, assisting in the development of their creativity, and also motivating them to work hard and give their best effort in everything possible.

    Current hobby

    • Watching my grandkids play sports
    • spending time/traveling with family and friends, as well as small projects around the house.

    Favorite Food

    • Green vegetables

    Favorite Movie/Book

    • Books by Tony Evans
