    Precious Birdsong

    Executive Director

    Precious Birdsong currently serves as the Assistant Director of Facilities and Game Operations at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, where she currently resides. Precious is a 2-time graduate from Middle Tennessee State University, where she obtained her BS in Leisure, Sport & Tourism Studies and MS in Sport Management.

    The Chattanooga native was also a 4-year softball student-athlete where she collected several accolades and helped lead the softball team to its first ever Conference USA Tournament championship title and second NCAA Regional Tournament appearance in program history.

    Beyond her work, Precious enjoys giving back in any capacity possible – hence, that is why she founded the Precious Birdsong Family Foundation to create a better future for people in the community of her hometown.
