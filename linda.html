    Linda R. Cooke

    Board Member

    Linda R. Cooke is the Director of Student Diversity and Development at Baylor School where she works closely with students and parents, building relationships and keeping lines of communication open.

    Linda currently resides in Chattanooga, TN, and a graduate of East Tennessee State University. Linda is a wife, a mother of two daughters, and a grandmother of four grandchildren.

    Beyond her work at Baylor, Linda is a mentor to many past students and coworkers. She is also a Golden Life Member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc. which is an organization whose purpose is to provide assistance and support through established programs in local communities throughout the world.
