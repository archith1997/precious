    Kelli H. Smith

    Board Member

    Kelli H. Smith serves as the Assistant Athletic Director and Head Softball Coach at Baylor School. Kelli currently resides in Chattanooga, TN with her husband and two sons.

    She received her BA in History from University of Tennessee at Chattanooga and her MEd in Education from Lincoln Memorial University. Kelli also attended Baylor School where her accolades began to pile up. As the Head Coach of the softball team, Kelli has earned numerous awards locally and nationally including Coach of the Year several years and many state championships.

    She also started the Hits for Hope softball tournament, raising $30,000 for the MaryEllen Lochler Foundation to help fight breast cancer and provide scholarships to students whose families have been affected by the disease.

    Passionate About

    • I love kids, I love the game, I love competition, and I love winning.
    • The greatest joy is helping young people learn the value of commitment, hard work, teamwork, and accountability in the development of their character - pushing them to excel without compromising doing what is right.
    • Seeing kids work hard to achieve individual and team goals while working through adversity on and off the field is very rewarding.
    • I take the responsibility and privilege of coaching young ladies very seriously and understand the impact I have on them.
    • I enjoy building relationships with my girls that continue after they leave Baylor.
    • Nothing is more exciting than watching them become successful in their careers, as wives, and as mothers.

    Current hobby

    • Watching my two sons play sports, and anything outdoors
