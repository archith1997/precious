    About Us

    • Our Mission

      The Precious Birdsong Family Foundation was founded on the grounds of giving back to the Chattanooga community. In keeping with our founder, Precious Birdsong and her family often make it a priority of theirs to give away clothes, food, toys, and other necessities to those in need in the Chattanooga community, especially during the holiday seasons.

    • Our Crew

      The PBFF Board of Directors represents those important champions that help guide and grow our programming. The work we do would not be possible without the support of these individuals. They are with us for the long haul, and they believe in our mission of bettering the community to produce a better tomorrow.
