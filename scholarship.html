    Scholarship

    To apply for this scholarship, a student/family must have already been granted acceptance into the Baylor School.

    With providing scholarships, it would be in the efforts of the Precious Birdsong Family Foundation to improve the children’s educational and life outcomes. While the criteria for the scholarship(s) are still being finalized, the recipients must meet standard testing requirements and fulfill a community service obligation. The Precious Birdsong Family Foundation will strive to redefine what a community and family looks like, and as we do so, we will continue to break barriers and open doors for the students and families in Chattanooga.

    Some of the criteria is as follows:

    • Academic Success – GPA requirement of 3.0
    • Financial Need
    • Participation in extra-curricular activities/sports
    • Personal Character
    • Leadership Roles
